about me

I am currently a Data Scientist at Getir. I work on several types of machine learning problems such as customer segmentation, churn prediction and sales forecasting.

I have recently successfully defended my PhD dissertation at Istanbul Technical University. My focus of research lies in the bridge between machine learning and Topological Data Analysis (TDA). I explore how topological features can be used for supervised learning.

I like to work on kaggle datasets. Sometimes I solve algorithm problems on leetcode and hackerrank.
