about me

I am currently a PhD candidate at Istanbul Technical University studying Topological Data Analysis (TDA). My focus of research lies in the bridge between machine learning and persistent homology. I explore how topological features can be used for supervised learning.

In my latest work, I used topological descriptors obtained via TDA for time series classification. In particular, I used TDA as a feature engineering method to distinguish physiological signals collected from human subjects under stressful and non-stressful events.

Sometimes I solve algorithm problems on leetcode and hackerrank. I also like to work on kaggle datasets.
